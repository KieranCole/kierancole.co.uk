My Work

Over the past few years at university, I've been able to work on a variety of projects that I can be proud of. These projects focused on different elements of web design and allowed me to control the entire life cycle of a project from planning to deployment. I've been able to explore different methodologies such as Flex-box and CSS Grids, both of which have changed the way I approach a site build.

Since university, I've been working across multiple projects to aid my learning. I've recently completed a freelance project for an MMU Art student, designing and building them a new portfolio site. Over the next few months, I will be working with Yellow Team, an organisation who connect volunteers with charities who need help from the digital community. I'm working towards entering the digital industry full time and developing further.
